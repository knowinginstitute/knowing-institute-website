Let’s agree upon what we are talking about.

Protocol for saving us from ourselves (and from future ai)

The Knowing Protocol offers a common ground for understanding a world created by interconnected agents. It can be used as a framework for sense-making, and as a shared protocol for accurately transfer meaning between humans and machines.

It is a single exhaustive framework for representing individual and collective sense-making, accumulating knowledge, based on a distributed graph-ledger technology.

Knowing Institute protects the protocol and coordinates the continuous growth of content.

What is it for?

There are two main reasons for the protocol.

> Bootstrap Democracy

Rapid technological development puts a lot of stress on democratic systems. In order for democracy to work, a majority of us must learn how to handle a much greater complexity. Unfortunately, it is going to take some time for educational systems to transform a global society. Even an optimistic prognosis would say it takes at least another 2-3 generations. And it doesn’t take a pessimist to think that might be too late.

Traditionally there have been very few suggestions to solve this problem, leaving us with some kind of top-down societal power for controlling our minds, either with brute-force, ideology or mabye a strong AI in the future.

Knowing Protocol is a way of hacking the system. Using The procofol as a reference for ideas and concept would give us transparence in how different interest groups define problems and solutions. Combining with some kind of Personal Ai, helping anyone to analyze complex information, would give more people the ability to familiarize themselves with complex problems. Combining AI with The Protocol would offer a way of creating a better democracy upside down rather than creating control upside down.

> Bootstrap Symbolic AI

Once promising, objectives ragarding Symbolic AI has made much resarch abonding it in recent yeas, to focus on Deep Learning's ability to create models representing human knowledge. The Protocol addresses these weak spots and offers soulutions. Further more, it stresses the importance of using traceablility and natural languge in any application which will have some effect on how humans create meaning of life and society.
